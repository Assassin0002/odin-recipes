Homemade Melt-In-Your-Mouth Dark Chocolate

Description

Learn how to make chocolate that melts in your mouth!
This homemade chocolate recipe is made without artificial ingredients or waxes and is ideal for health-conscious individuals who enjoy an indulgent treat.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup coconut oil
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Gently melt coconut oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla extract into melted oil until well blended.
  3. Pour the mixture into a candy mold or pliable tray.
  4. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.
